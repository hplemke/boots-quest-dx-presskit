Boots Quest DX

About

Boots Quest DX is a video game by Discount Realms (Harrison Lemke). It's a humorous, nonviolent adventure game that imagines a goofy children's-book fantasy RPG world where Boots are the only equipment that matters. It focuses on story, exploration, and environmental puzzle-solving; it's a bit like a point-and-click adventure but it feels nicer to control. And you can jump.

History

Boots Quest was born when a friend told Harrison that they enjoyed a small game he'd made because "it wasn't like i had to read up on the stats of 17 different kinds of boots before i started to get anything out of it". That sounded like a good idea for a game, and Harrison has been working on it ever since. It's influenced by anti-RPGs like Space Funeral, slow-paced indie exploration games of yesteryear like Knytt, as well as more obvious touchstones like Dragon Quest, the Legend of Zelda and various LucasArts adventure titles of yore.

Features

  • A compact open world full of gremlins, dragons, seagulls, wizards, and weird folks of all sorts
  • 20+ hats and 40+ boots to wear, plus a bunch of other items to collect
  • A story mode you can finish in 1-2 sittings, plus numerous side quests, bonus challenges, and secrets
  • Relaxing gameplay that's fun for all skill levels
  • A New Game+ mode for hardcore boot enthusiasts
  • Wiggly hand-drawn art
  • Atmospheric music and sound, including dynamic footstep sounds

About Discount Realms

Discount Realms is a small studio whose mission is to provide quality digital Realms to the budget-minded consumer. It consists solely of Harrison Lemke, a multimedia artist and neurotic presence from the Austin, TX area who elicits rare glimmers of recognition due to his prior work as a chiptune and indie rock musician.
